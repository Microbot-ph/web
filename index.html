About

What we do:

At Microbot, we provide innovative electronics, robotics, and STEM learning solutions designed for students, hobbyists, makers, educators, and professionals.

Our mission is to make technology education more accessible through hands-on learning experiences that inspire creativity, problem-solving, and innovation.

Embedded Systems Development

Custom microcontroller-based systems tailored for automation, robotics, IoT, and intelligent devices.

Board-Level Circuit Design

From schematic design to PCB development, we engineer efficient and production-ready electronic hardware.

Learning and Prototyping Platforms

Hands-on electronics kits and development boards that simplify embedded systems learning without sacrificing technical depth.

Website Development

Modern, responsive, and performance-focused websites engineered for businesses, technical projects, and digital platforms with clean UI, scalable architecture, and seamless user experience.


Our Commitment:

We believe that the best way to learn technology is by building it. That's why we focus on products that allow users to assemble, program, test, and understand how modern electronics work from the ground up.

Whether you're building your first circuit, developing a robotics project, or creating your next innovation, Microbot is here to support your journey.
